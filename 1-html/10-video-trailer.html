Mr.Robot

Mr. Robot is an American TV series that follows Elliot Alderson, a brilliant but troubled cybersecurity engineer and hacker. He’s recruited by a mysterious anarchist called Mr. Robot to join a group of hacktivists known as fsociety, who aim to bring down corrupt corporations. The show dives deep into themes like mental health, identity, surveillance, and digital control, blending psychological drama with cyber-thriller elements. It’s known for its clever writing, plot twists, and realistic portrayal of hacking.
